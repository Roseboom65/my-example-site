Hi. On this page I will tell you a little more about myself.

I learned to overcome adversity at a very young age, as I needed to have open-heart surgery at the age of 4 to repair a hole in my heart and a leaky valve. Additionally, when I was 18, I had a stroke that completely paralyzed the left side of my body. As scary as that sounds, it saved my life as the doctors at the hospital discovered a second hole in my heart that would have killed me by 23 if it was left unrepaired. Thus, I had my second open-heart surgery at the age of 18.

While recovering from my stroke and surgery, I met and began to date my future husband Steve. We got married in Las Vegas in October 2001.

We were living in Arizona, but in 2003, we decided that the climate here was more agreeable in Utah, so made the move here to Salt Lake City.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and playing video games, as well as watching TV and movies.

I also own a 2020 Polaris Rzr as well as a camper with a deck (so we can take the Rzr with us), and several times in the summer, we enjoy going to the mountains to go camping and ATV riding.
